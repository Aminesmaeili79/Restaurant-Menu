What type of potatoes do you use to make our fries? Dear Sandra, in Europe our French fries are made from whole, real potatoes. Myria

Do you wear gloves? Yes Myria, our crew wears gloves – in addition to hand washing - when handling and preparing food. We have implemented a two colored glove system to avoid cross contamination. Blue colored gloves are worn when handling raw meat and eggs, and clear gloves are worn when handling cooked products and ready to eat ingredients like salad, cheeses, buns.

Nicolas

Do you wash your hands? Dear Nicolas, at McDonald's we apply rigorous standards of food safety and hygiene standards. All crew, no matter what station or task they are working on in the restaurant, must wash their hands before starting work and at least every hour throughout their shift and have strict orders to stay clean and take very good care of personal hygiene. All of our crew members are trained on hand washing procedures using anti-microbial soap specifically developed for McDonald’s.

Philip

Do you clean your oil? How many times you change it? Dear Philip, proper Oil management is very important for oil quality. This includes oil filtering, cleaning of vats, temperature control, refilling and covering vats if they are not in use to avoid water and salt getting into the oil. In McDonald's restaurants' oil quality is checked every day for every vat and may be changed depending on optical appearance, taste of products and chemical parameter.

George

Is your ice-cream lactose free? Dear George, our ice cream is not lactose free. It does contain fresh milk, cream and skimmed milk powder.

Ramona

Do your burgers contain soya? Dear Ramona, we appreciate that some of our customers have allergies. Therefore, we develop our products to have a technical minimum of allergens. However, we always highlight that cross contact between food items is always a risk in any working kitchen, therefore we cannot guarantee that any food items are soy-free even if soy is not an ingredient. We are providing information to customers about potential allergens in our food for a number of years. This information is available on our website, as well as in our restaurants (Allergen Information Table).

Anna

Do any of your products contain nuts? Dear Anna, we are aware of the serious health risk to people who are allergic to whole peanuts, nuts or nut derivatives and these are excluded as ingredients from our menu items. However, we are unable to categorically state that our products are 100% free of nuts or nut derivatives, because there is a possibility of cross contamination at some point in the food chain or even in our restaurants. In the event that a promotional product contains peanuts, nuts, or nuts derivatives, this will be clearly labelled on the restaurants allergen information. Aside from that we provide ingredient and allergen information on our websites.
